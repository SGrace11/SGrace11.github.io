Design Document

Project Overview

  • Application Description: A website to show items for bid and offer background on the company.
  • Intended Users: The users of this site will be people looking to browse through and purchase clothes from Wildflower Finds and the owners of Wildflower Finds.
  • Content Overview: The website will have an area for clothing listings and reviews, information about the business and its owners, and a form for a personal shopping opportunity.

Page Design

  • Index
    • Purpose: welcome the user to the site
    • Users: customers
    • Content: a welcome message, business logo, general explanation of item organization
    • Users Entering Data? No
    • Data Validation Needed? N/A
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks/Dropdowns? Yes
    • Actions Possible: navigate to new page
    • Special Notes: N/A
  • About
    • Purpose: inform the user of the history of the business and who the owners are
    • Users: customers
    • Content: message from the owners about themselves and business origin
    • Users Entering Data? No
    • Data Validation Needed? N/A
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks/Dropdowns? Yes
    • Actions Possible: navigate to new page, navigate away from site
    • Special Notes: N/A
  • Items
    • Purpose: show the user the items for sale
    • Users: customers
    • Content: pictures and breif discriptions of items for sale
    • Users Entering Data? Yes
    • Data Validation Needed? Yes
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks/Dropdowns? Yes
    • Actions Possible: navigate to new page, dropdown info, submit a value, filter items
    • Special Notes: later development will connect site to a database
  • News
    • Purpose: inform the user of upcoming sales/events and social news from the owners
    • Users: customers
    • Content: announcements of sales and blog-like posts from owners
    • Users Entering Data? No
    • Data Validation Needed? N/A
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks/Dropdowns? Yes
    • Actions Possible: navigate to new page, expand div to see more info
    • Special Notes: N/A
  • Form
    • Purpose: gather info from users who are interested in a personal thrifting box
    • Users: customers
    • Content: information about personal thrifting service and form for user information related to service
    • Users Entering Data? Yes
    • Data Validation Needed? Yes
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks/Dropdowns? Yes
    • Actions Possible: navigate to new page, submit form data, clear form data
    • Special Notes: later development will connect site to a database

Dynamic Functionality

  • Items info in accordion sections
  • News in accordion sections
  • Form to collect user data
